Features

The world isn't fit for otters anymore and you, as the one and only choosen otter, have to enter otter space and get acces to new worlds to live in. On the go you collect information and trees. Information is needed to get on, speeding up your yourney and getting news and fresh trees from otter space back to your beloved ones on planet earth.

/Setting

Flying out to otter space means endless possiblities. Meaning that in space there is enough room to add new minigames, all being part of the great space adventure. And adding more stuff worth learning.

/Educative

Making it educational was our goal from the very start of the project. Making it cool to get involved with interesting content. Content that helps you earning high scores, or even the highscore.

/Online

You're not the only otter in otter space. You're not out there all alone! There are many otters online. Not only trying to set the best score, but also adding their personal content to the platform.
