About

Introduction

Our journey began in September of 2014. Marc, manager of CortexArts, reached out to one of the teachers at a local school. This is how the first members of the team got in contact with Marc and started to meet up in so called "sessions."

Our goal

Since then we dream of gathering a community to develop a serious game for public education. This way we wanted to battle the boredom some students having during school, and make it more entertaining and interactable.
