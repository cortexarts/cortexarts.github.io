Our work

Wij zijn Cortexarts, een groep studenten met één doel: huiswerk volledig veranderen. Cortexarts is in 2014 opgericht, toen wij nog op de middelbare school zaten. Iedereen in ons team had moeite met huiswerk maken en wij vonden dat dit anders moest kunnen. Met dit idee was CortexArts geboren. Wij willen games integreren in het huiswerk van middelbare scholieren, zodat zij zonder dat het opvalt toch de leerstof voor proefwerken kunnen leren. Wij geloven dat, met behulp van de magie van games, huiswerk erg leuk kan zijn!

Your work

We believe that crowdsourcing is the best way to approach such a big project. That's why our work has been open-source since the beginning. This means that anyone who wants to contribute to the project can do that without any limitations. Besides that, GitHub gives us a platform to analyse and update our progress from wherever we want. This makes it even easier for anyone to work on the future of education.

Our goal

We want everyone to get ready for the next generation of learning. In the coming years we would like to start a community with educative content that is not limited by strict and repetitive curricula and syllabi. We want to provide public education with a platform that provides gameplay, not only made by ourselves, but by the player.
