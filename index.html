What we do

We create gameplay, design characters, draw backgrounds, animate storylines, motivate eachother, plug in serious content into awesome crazy games, loose ourselves in all kinds of exercises in virtual gymnastics (What games are not explored?)

What you can do

Our target group of players is any student, either wanting to play games that deploy serious content, or students wanting to create learningobjects in game environments. We build an innumerous amount of crazy games filled with things you always wanted to know but never got the chance to learn! We have reason to believe that learning, using the force of gaming, is so much more fun, that we made it our mission to provide education with a platform that provides gameplay and enables developing them yourselves.

What we want

We want the entire learning community to get ready for the next gen in learning. It is time to finish the arrogant monopoly of learning content developers and start a community that is not restricted by curricula and Syllabi telling what has to be learned. We find great satisfaction in creating new realities and new science by being part of it
