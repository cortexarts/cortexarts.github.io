About

Introduction

Our journey began in September of 2014. Marc, manager of CortexArts, reached out to one of the teachers at a local school. This is how the first members of the team got in contact with Marc and started to meet up during sessions.

Since then we dream of gathering a community to develop serious games for public education. This way we wanted to battle the boredom some students have during school, and make it more entertaining.
